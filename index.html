HOW TO PLAY

How To:

  1. Playing "War" is very simple, both parties have a deck of cards.
  2. To draw a card from your deck just click on it.
  3. You win the round if your card has a higher value than your opponent's card. (Number card take their numeric value. However for royal cards: Jack = 10, Queen = 11, King = 12, Ace = 13)
  4. To win the game you must win 5 rounds.
