HOW TO PLAY

How To:

  1. To draw a card from your deck just click on it.
  2. You win the round if your card has a higher value than your opponent's card. (Number cards take their numeric value. However for royal cards: Jack = 10, Queen = 11, King = 12, Ace = 13)
  3. In the case of a tie, the round is a draw and players draw again for 2 points.
  4. To win the game you must win 10 points.
